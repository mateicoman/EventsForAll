About us

Events for all is an easy to use, friendly and accessible website, which has been created to make it easier for the parents/careres of children with disabilities to find events, fairs, groups, companies and more which are suited for their needs. Users can review companies and events, and look at the reviews of others, so that they can check whether something is suitable, wihtout relying on a company's possibly biased viewpoint.


Why have we created this website?

Many parents and carers find it very difficult to find things for their children to do out in the commnity, or they find that there is a lack of useful information. Events For All ensures that fantastic opportunities and detailed information is available all in one place.
